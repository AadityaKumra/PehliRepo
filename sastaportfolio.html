My SWOT Analysis

I am Aaditya Kumra from Kota, Rajasthan. What I have introspected from my entire school journey is that I have taken part in almost all activities that my school life could offer, whether be it academics, sports, cultural performance, quizzes, debating or NCC and I feel very happy that I have not missed a single opportunity throughout. This participation helped me a lot in finding my hobbies and most importantly it told me that all it takes is a little effort, enthusiasm and determination to achieve something in life. Talking about my hobbies, my hobbies are playing football and table tennis and I have represented till state level in both the sports. I love reading books but I am very specific about what kind of books I want to read. Besides this I love to swim and cycle. The recent habit that I have developed in this pandemic is binge watching movies and TV series. From these hobbies and participations, I learnt a lot about my strengths and weaknesses. I can say that my strength is that I never give up once I have decided to do something. And at the back of my mind I have that confidence and spark that I have, I can and I will achieve whatever I want to even if I feel a little hesitant about it in the beginning. I remember that almost a year back I had this urge to cycle for long distances, so I gradually started and was able to cycle for around 50kms back in the day. What helps me go a step ahead is that I don’t try to find reasons outside, rather I build up that motivation inside me. Talking about my weaknesses, my weakness is that if I have to complete a task in a given period of time, what I do is I try to find out ways so that I can start and finish the task at the last stretch of the given period and save the rest of my time in doing something else. Sometimes what happens is that it leads to procrastination of the task. My other weakness is that I tend to find it difficult to mix up with people in the beginning. It takes a lot of effort for me to enter conversations, but the best part is that when I make friends with them or become accustomed to talk to them, then I can mingle a lot. Speaking about threats and risk analysis the basic concept which I follow in each aspect of my life is to keep my eyes and mind open and analyze before taking a step ahead, if I find an obstacle, I try to keep a cool and calm mind and approach the problem with an optimistic perspective. During this tough pandemic time, since the last 6 months we have taken a lot of precautions at our home, we follow strict rules, we sanitize and quarantine the things brought from outside. I myself have gone out only a couple of times due to unavoidable circumstances only, you can even see that I haven’t visited the barber since the pandemic. Sometimes I feel frustrated for taking all these precautions when I see my friends and other people meeting each other, shopping and not caring about catching the virus , but then I explain myself that I have a grandparent at home who is an asthmatic patient and he needs to be protected from all angles and I should avoid getting him involved in any kind of risks. I always set my priorities and try to work accordingly.
Thanks a lot
